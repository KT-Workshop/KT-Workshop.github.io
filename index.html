11th International Ljubljana-Zagreb workshop on Knowledge Technologies and Data Science

24th - 25th October 2019, Zagreb, Croatia

Welcome to the webpage of the Knowledge Technologies and Data Science Workshop! The objective of this workshop is to present current research topics in the broad field of knowledge technologies, stimulate cooperation on ongoing projects, and enable team building for future project proposals. Participation is by invitation only. The workshop is held at the Faculty of electrical engineering and computing, Zagreb, Croatia.

Workshop programme

The workshop consists of individual presentations, group presentations, discussions and networking events. We hope that the program will stimulate individuals in their research work and enable them to contribute as much as possible to the group vision for future research.

Organization

The workshop is organized by the Centre for Research Excellence for Data Science and Advanced Cooperative Systems, research unit for data science, Croatia, in cooperation with Rudjer Boskovic Institute, Croatia, and Jozef Stefan Institute, Slovenia.
